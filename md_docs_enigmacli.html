enigmacli

A CLI Enigma simulator.

Usage

enigmacli [-s plugboard] [-w rotors] [-p rotor_positions] [-u reflector]

enigmacli reads from stdin and outputs to stdout by default, and is case-agnostic.

The following options are supported:
